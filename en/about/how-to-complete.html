Eligibility

Any current or recent student may register as a competitor.. A current student is defined as a person of any age that is enrolled in a recognized academic institution. A recent student would have been a current student until 3 years ago (5 July 2017). An individual may be attached to an academic institution by being ever enrolled in it. A recognized institution is an accredited educational institution or an institution that may grant a degree or diploma. All institutions and competitors must be registered with the IBCOL Registrar Office.

Submission

Projects are submitted by teams of one to six eligible competitors. Each team must nominate one representative academic institution, if any of the team members are from different academic institutions. This representative institution determines which country or territory the team and thus project will represent. Teams may be composed of members from different countries/territories or academic institutions. A team may submit more than one project. The Hong Kong Blockchain Olympiad accepts project submissions on a rolling basis with a hard deadline in the 1st of June 2020 at 23:59 HKT.

Project Ideas

Below are probable examples. Participants are free to choose their own application area.

  • Identity Application Domain: E-KYC, Reputation System, Birth Registration, Patent Identification
  • Finance Application Domain: Pension, Payment, Stock Market, Subsidies
  • Land Application Domain: Mutation, Registration, Verification
  • Expatriates Application Domain: Employment, Welfare, Remittance
  • Agriculture Application Domain: Procurement, Disintermediation, Agricultural Insurance, Agricultural product provenance
  • Commerce Application Domain: Consumer Protection, VAT, Fraud Detection of Consumer Products
  • Health Application Domain: Privacy-preserving collection, Storage and retrieval of health data
  • Banking/Insurance Application Domain: Digital Asset Management, Inter-bank Settlement, Trade finance (back-to-back L/C), P2P Lending, Anti-Money Laundering, Digital Currency, Credit rating, Insurance Policy Authentication
  • Judiciary Application Domain: Securing Digital Evidence
  • Supply Chain Application Domain: Agricultural Supply Chain, Medicine Supply Chain
  • Document Verification Application Domain: Government Certificate, Educational Certificate
  • Smart City Application Domain: Smart Infrastructures, Citizen Services
  • Manufacturing Application Domain: Garments, RMG products or manufacturing goods provenance
  • Infrastructure: Protection of Critical Information Infrastructure
